About the photographer

I’m Karina. My story oddly begins as a food scientist for seven years. Although that career path was a wonderful time in my life, I finally got the courage to pursue my true passion - photography! I was able to go from science to photography and am now a lifestyle photographer and super proud mommy of one!

Nothing gives me more joy than capturing love stories, family milestone and beautiful memories. I would describe my style as light, airy and fresh. I love sun-lit fields, golden sun flare and creating images that capture you and in a way you'll want to hold on to and always cherish. A session with me consists of candid, romantic, and free spirited moments. Genuinely fun times = lovely pictures! I believe in using natural light and settings for sessions. I love shooting in all types of natural settings, the rarer the better. I am always up for a challenge and absolutely love when my clients are up for interesting and unique concepts. Those usually make for the best and most fun sessions! My goal is to make your photos feel full of life and light.

I am currently serving the NJ/NY/PA area. From family portraits to pet portraits, let’s get in touch – I can’t wait to hear your story and help preserve moments in time that you will have for the rest of your life!

Lots of love,

Karina Scheren
