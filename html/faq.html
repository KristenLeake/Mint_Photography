How do I book an appointment?

We love to do a session consultation to chat about the details of your session, get to know each other a bit and talk about what kind of images you’re looking for in session.

Do you travel for photoshoots and events?

I currently serve the New Jersey, New York and Pennsylvania area but am absolutely willing to travel for destination shoots. Let me know where and we will work out the details from there!

What form of payment do you accept?

Cash, Check, PayPal & Credit Cards – Processing fees and Sales Tax Apply.

How soon after our session will our images be available?

Sneak peeks are available typically the next day. An online gallery of your images will be available approximately 2-3 weeks after your session. You will also receive a USB in the mail with all your edited high-resolution files.

What happens if it rains on the day of my outdoor session?

In the event of rain during a scheduled outdoor session, we will either utilize an indoor area or reschedule for a different date.

Where do the portrait sessions take place?

I have great locations that I’ve scouted out and are perfect for photo sessions. For family and children shoots you can choose between urban, country or anything in between. Newborn Sessions take place in your home. Tell me your ideas, I’m always open to new locations. If you need ideas, that works too! Just ask us! Please book your session at least 3-4 weeks in advance to allow for session availability.

How long does a typical session last?

Typically sessions last between 1-3 hours depending on the type of session you book and the number of locations we are shooting at.
